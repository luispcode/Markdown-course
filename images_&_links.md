## Link structure:

##### \[text](link)

**For example:**

\![Go to EDteam]\(https://ed.team)

[Go to EDteam](https://ed.team)

##### Second option:
Define a text with a link [EDteam] to redirect to a webpage.

**Using the syntax:**
\[Edteam]:https://ed.team

[Edteam]:https://ed.team
